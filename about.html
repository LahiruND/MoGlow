About Us

The Art of Illumination

Our Beginning

MoGlow began with a simple passion: to create an atmosphere of serenity in a chaotic world. Started in a small kitchen in Colombo, our journey has been fueled by the obsession to craft the perfect candle. We believe that a candle is not just wax and wick, but a vessel for memories and moods.

Our Craft

We prioritize quality above all else. Every MoGlow candle is hand-poured using 100% natural soy wax and lead-free cotton wicks. We source premium fragrances that are free from phthalates and parabens, ensuring a clean burn that is safe for you and your family.
